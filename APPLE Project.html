IPHONE 15 PRO & PRO MAX

The iPhone "Pro" is equipped with the same port, in addition to being "the first iPhone made of titanium used in the field of aviation and space." The phone runs on the A17 PRO processor chip used in the previous “Pro” version. Both Pro and Pro Max will be released in four new colors, including titanium black, titanium white, titanium blue, and natural titanium.The price of the iPhone 15 starts at $799, the “Plus” from $899, the “Pro” from $999, and the “Pro Max” from $1,199.
